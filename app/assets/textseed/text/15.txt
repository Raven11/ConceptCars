			<h2>Rolls Royce 200EX</h2>
				
			<p>An update of a classic, the 200EX brings Rolls Royce into the 21st century. Its toned-down style is a bit less stately than its predecessors and the body is much smaller, but designers in the BMW division retain such traditional Rolls Royce designs as aft-hinged rear doors.<br> </p>
