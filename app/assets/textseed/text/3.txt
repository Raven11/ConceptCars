			<h2>Audi Sportback</h2>
				
			<p>This preview of the Audi (<a href="http://stockmarket.businessweek.com/www/search.html?q=VOWG">VOWG</a>) A7 takes the coupe's four-door simplicity and adds a hatchback, a glass dome, and LED strip headlights that seem to be winking. Its low, single-frame body keeps the center of gravity close to the road and looks as if it were drawn in a single stroke.<br> </p>
