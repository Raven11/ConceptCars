			<h2>Koenigsegg Quant</h2>
				
			<p>This Swedish company's sports car runs on solar power&mdash;its skin is coated in photovoltaic film, letting it travel 300 miles on a 20-minute charge. The dramatic front fenders, incognito windshield, and enormous side intakes are straight from the future, but its molded lines are rooted in the past.<br> </p>
