			<h2>Cadillac Converj</h2>
				
			<p>The Converj could be a sneak peek at General Motors' electric future. It sports the grille of newer Caddies while its upturned rear reminds us of older models. Head and tail lights have been turned into slits. And is that a javelin or a giant Nike swoosh on each side panel?<br> </p>
