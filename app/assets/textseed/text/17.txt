			<h2>Jeep Renegade</h2>
				
			<p>Jeep has its roots in rugged all-purpose vehicles, and they show in the Renegade. This topless two-seater has the flared wheel openings and oversized tires of a dune buggy. But it's not entirely a 1960s throwback: The dual-fuel car claims 110 mpg from its Bluetec diesel engine and electric motors.<br> </p>
